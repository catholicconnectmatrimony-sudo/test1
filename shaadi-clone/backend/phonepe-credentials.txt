PHONEPE INTEGRATION CREDENTIALS
===============================

To activate PhonePe payments, please provide the following credentials:

1. PHONEPE_MERCHANT_ID: [Your PhonePe Merchant ID]
2. PHONEPE_SALT_KEY: [Your PhonePe Salt Key]  
3. PHONEPE_SALT_INDEX: [Usually 1]
4. PHONEPE_ENVIRONMENT: [UAT for testing, PROD for production]

INSTRUCTIONS:
============

1. Get your PhonePe merchant account at: https://www.phonepe.com/business-solutions/
2. Once approved, you'll receive:
   - Merchant ID
   - Salt Key
   - Salt Index (usually 1)

3. Update the .env file with your credentials:
   PHONEPE_MERCHANT_ID=your_actual_merchant_id
   PHONEPE_SALT_KEY=your_actual_salt_key
   PHONEPE_SALT_INDEX=1
   PHONEPE_ENVIRONMENT=UAT

4. For testing, use UAT environment
5. For production, change to PROD environment

TESTING:
=======
- UAT environment allows testing with fake payments
- Test cards and UPI IDs are provided by PhonePe for UAT
- No real money is charged in UAT mode

PRODUCTION:
==========
- Change PHONEPE_ENVIRONMENT=PROD
- Real payments will be processed
- Ensure merchant account is fully verified

Please provide your PhonePe credentials to activate the payment system!
